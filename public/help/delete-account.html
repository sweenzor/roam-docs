Delete Account

markdown version · view in Roam Research

  • Deleting your account deletes every graph you're the only admin of, along with the files uploaded to them.
    • Graphs that have other admins aren't deleted. Your access is removed instead, and the graph stays with them.
  • You lose access immediately. Full deletion of your data may take up to 30 days.
  • Export your notes first: see Export.

How to delete your account

  • Open Settings → Account → Danger Zone → Delete my account.
  • Type your email address to confirm, then click I understand the consequences, delete my account.
  • You can also delete your account from the mobile app's account settings.
