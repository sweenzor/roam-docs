Command Palette

markdown version · view in Roam Research

  • One searchable list of just about everything Roam can do: open it, type a few letters, hit Enter.
  • It's the fastest route to an action when you don't know (or don't remember) its hotkey.

How to use it

  • Press Cmd-p / Ctrl-p anywhere.
  • Type part of a command's name to filter the list. A word or two is enough, in any order.
  • ↑ / ↓ to choose, Enter to run, Esc to close.
  • When it closes, your cursor returns to the block you were writing in.

What you'll find inside

  • App-wide actions: toggle the sidebars, open today's Daily Notes, Advanced Search, Global search, Zen Mode, the Graph Overview, and more.
  • Housekeeping: import, export and backups, attachments, Check for updates, and Chat with Roam support.
  • Settings: … commands that jump straight to a specific section of your settings.
  • Edit Hotkey: … commands that take you to that command in Settings → Hotkeys, so you can give it keys of your own.
  • Roam Depot: the marketplace and extension settings. Installed extensions can add commands of their own to the list.

Good to know

  • Commands that have a hotkey show it on the right, so the palette doubles as a hotkey cheat sheet.
  • In Zen Mode, Toggle Zen Mode is pinned to the top of the list, so the way out is always one Cmd-p away.
