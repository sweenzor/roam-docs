All Graphs Page

markdown version · view in Roam Research

  • The All Graphs page lists every graph you have. It's Roam's home screen, and where new graphs get created.
  • You land on it when you sign in at roamresearch.com.
  • From inside a graph, the graph switcher at the top of the Left Sidebar takes you between graphs and back to the full list.

Hosted and local graphs

  • A Hosted Graph is synchronized with Roam's servers and accessible from anywhere.
  • A Local Graph lives in that browser's storage, on that computer only.
  • Back up local graphs regularly using export: clearing your browser's data deletes them.

Creating a graph

  • Click + Create Graph, then pick Create Hosted Graph or Create Local Graph.
  • Choose the name carefully. It must be unique, and it becomes the graph's permanent URL (hosted) or storage name (local).
  • Tick Encrypted to create an [encrypted graph](Encrypted Graphs). This is decided at creation; an existing graph can't be encrypted later.
  • If you lose an encrypted graph's password, your data cannot be recovered, because Roam never has the key.
  • Creating a hosted graph needs an internet connection.

The account menu

  • Click your name in the top right to open the account menu.
  • Billing: manage your subscription and payment details.
  • Check for Updates: get the newest version of Roam right away. See Updating Roam.
  • Theme: Light, Dark, or Auto (System). This only changes Roam outside your graphs; inside a graph, appearance is customized with Roam Depot extensions or roam/css.
  • Sign Out: signs you out of Roam on this device.

Good to know

  • The search box filters your list, handy once the graphs pile up.
  • The Download Desktop App button in the top right gets you the standalone app. See Install Roam for more options.
