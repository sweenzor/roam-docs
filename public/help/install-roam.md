# Install Roam

### Install Roam Mobile ([[iOS]] and [[Android]])

- [[iOS]]
  - download [Roam Mobile on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/roam-mobile/id1609277273)
- [[Android]]: 
  - download [Roam on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.roamresearch.relemma)
- Sign in with your Roam account and your graphs will be there. [[Roam Mobile]] covers what the app can do.

### Install Roam Desktop ([[Mac]], [[Windows]], and [[Linux]])

- The [[Desktop App]] is a standalone version of Roam with its own windows and `roam://` deeplinks.
- Go to [roamresearch.com](https://roamresearch.com) and click the download button. It offers the right build for your computer.
- Builds for all platforms (macOS Apple Silicon and Intel, Windows, and Linux) are listed in the **Downloads** section further down the page.
