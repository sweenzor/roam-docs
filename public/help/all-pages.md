# All Pages

- Every page in your graph in one sortable list, with columns for mentions, word count, and when each page was created and last updated.
- Open it from **All Pages** in the left sidebar, or run **Open all pages** from the [[Command Palette]].

### Finding and sorting

- Type in the search box to filter the list.
- Sort by title, date updated, date created, mentions, or word count, in either direction.
- Choose which columns to show with **Show or Hide Columns**, and show or hide daily notes with the calendar toggle.
- Click a page to open it in the main window, or shift-click to open it in the [[Right Sidebar]] and keep the list where you are.

### Bulk actions

- Tick the checkboxes to select pages. The header checkbox selects everything in the current view.
- **Delete Selected Pages** and **Export Selected Pages** act on your whole selection at once.
- Good for cleanup: pages with no content show in italics as __Blank__, and sorting by word count, smallest to largest, brings them to the top.
