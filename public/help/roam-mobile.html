Roam Mobile

markdown version · view in Roam Research

  • Roam Mobile is the native app for iPhone, iPad, and Android. Install Roam has the store links.
  • Sign in with your Roam account and your graphs are there, in sync with everywhere else you use Roam.

The native view

  • The app's native view is built for phones: it feels native, opens fast, and handles large graphs without trouble.
  • You can edit in it, drag blocks around, react with emoji, and use queries, tables, sliders, block references, and callouts.
  • For things the native view doesn't cover yet, the app can open the full Roam web app.
  • Encrypted graphs aren't available in the native view, because the servers it reads from can't decrypt them. They open in the web app view instead.

Quick Capture

  • Quick Capture is for getting a thought down fast: open the app, type, done.
  • Captured notes land on your Daily Notes page the next time the graph syncs.
  • The capture template is customizable in the app's settings, so captures can arrive pre-tagged and pre-structured.
  • Encrypted graphs don't appear in Quick Capture's graph picker.

Good to know

  • Encrypted graphs can unlock with Face ID or your fingerprint instead of typing the password.
  • Links to Roam pages and blocks can open directly in the app.
